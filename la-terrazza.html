About us

A warm embrace and an exciting journey to a true oasis of comfort and Italian cuisine. La Terrazza Hotel & Restaurant is a union where wonderful comfort and authentic Italian flavors are perfectly intertwined.

RESTAURANT

The restaurant has capacity for 200 people and offers a rich culinary experience with Italian Mediterranean food. The menu includes the best products of Italian cuisine. The ambiance of the bar is modern and suitable for a pleasant experience. The chef has a long experience of 36 years in Italy, guaranteed for authentic taste and high quality in cooking.


Conference Rooms (up to 130 persons)

The conference hall at “La Terrazza” hotel in Hani i Elezit is a spacious venue accommodating up to 100 people, ideal for large meetings and corporate events. Equipped with high-speed internet, projectors, screens, and work tables with chairs, it ensures efficient and effective presentations. Additionally, there’s a relaxing space for breaks. La Terrazza also offers premium catering services, keeping attendees energized and focused.


DOLCE VITA

The pastry shop “Dolce Vita” is a paradise of flavors and aromas, where you can enjoy fresh food, wonderful desserts, and baked goods. A wonderful destination to indulge in an unforgettable taste.
